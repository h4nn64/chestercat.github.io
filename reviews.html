JUNNY

Song: Thank You

Although being an older song of Korean-American artist Junny, Thank You still caught the ears of Chester Cat with its R&B vibes. Listeners can enjoy this chill music while studying or taking their daily walks, and still enjoy a boost of energy with its catchy rhythmic. Junny talks about how appreciative he is of his fans and listeners, but the song can be implied for anyone thanking others' support. Junny also demonstrates his wide vocal range as he sings charismatically lower in verses and high during the choruses. Chester Cat warns that despite this being a nice song to groove to, it may not be everyone's cup of tea. However, Chester Cat still recommends everyone to give this underrated song a chance!

DOJA CAT

Song: Woman

Woman is the opening song from Doja Cat's newest album Planet Her. The track incorporates afrobeats with modern pop, creating a unique sound behind the song's celebration of femininity. Doja Cat expertly weaves her wittisisms with the empowering tune while exploring the niches of womanhood and what it means to her. "Woman" by Doja Cat does not fail to impress and gains Chester Cat's stamp of approval!

Billie Eilish

Song: Happier Than Ever

Happier Than Ever is Billie's title song from her second studio album. Incorporating two different music genres, the first half of the song follows a 4/4 beat with a gentle flow using an acoustic guitar and Billie's soft voice. Towards the halfway mark, the instrumental transitions from acoustic to electric guitar and a 3/4 sway. Finneas O'Connell blends the two genres despite the duality between its lullaby and rock type halves. Billie expresses the lyrics of the song soulfully as she describes her experience of being emotionally healthier without someone who previously caused issues. Listeners will find themselves listening more and more to this song as its catchy transition becomes more familiar. Incredibly well written, Happier Than Ever gains Chester Cat's stamp of approval.
